What is UDभव?

UDBHAV is a Social-Emotional Learning Program working with children, parents, and primary caregivers in under-resourced communities across Delhi.

We envision to build resilience and emotional literacy to create a more safe and compassionate world for all of us.

Why UDभव?

The Socio-Economic culture around the child overwhelms them with preconceived expectations and does not allow them to cultivate their inherent qualities and skills. Which reflects:

AMONG CHILDREN AS
Children lack self-awareness which leads to difficulty in managing emotions, thoughts and a decrease in confidence which leads to emotional distress and sometimes aggression.

AMONG CAREGIVERS/PARENTS AS
They don't feel empowered enough to manage behaviour issues of children and often showed rampant complaints, violent behaviour, ignorance and nagging towards children.

Vision

Every child gets the opportunity to cultivate Social- Emotional Learning for their holistic development.

Mission

To empower children and caregivers to evolve Social-Emotional Learning among themselves and act as champions to enable others within their communities.
